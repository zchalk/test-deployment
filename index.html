FAQ's

Are any topics off limits? Yes!

Talks are thoughtful, not divisive:

  • No talks with an inflammatory political or religious agenda, nor polarizing "us vs them" language.
  • We seek to build consensus and provide outside-the-box thinking, not to revisit familiar, unresolvable disputes on these topics.
  • Speakers cannot promote their own products, books, or businesses, or those of a company which employs them.

If selected, can I use PowerPoint slides for my presentation?

Minimal. No more than 6 basic slides. The focus should be on you, not your PowerPoint slides. Watch the most viewed Ted Talks and you will see that slides are used sparingly.

This is a speech from the heart. You may not read or pre-record your speech.

How much does it cost to attend the event?

Admission is free. Seating is limited. Reservations required. We anticipate a sold-out event, so reserve early!

If selected, can I choose the order of when I speak?

No, the committee will determine the order of speakers.

What if I can't stay for the entire event?

Don't apply. It would be disrespectful to the rest of the speakers if you left before everyone spoke.

Will the event be recorded?

Yes, and your talk will be uploaded to YouTube.

Will the event be Livestreamed?

Yes. Link will be provided on the website.

If selected, you will be required to sign a media release form.
